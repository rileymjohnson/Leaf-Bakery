About Leaf

Leaf is a Five Star, High end restaurant serving you only the highest quality of food. We offer a wide variety of options, from healthy and delicious soups and salads, to your ever delicious deserts. We have won many awards, spread out through all our vast range of menu options. We even offer a Kids menu which is sure to satisfy your little ones cravings. Our wide range of drinks, from our adult-only cocktails and mixed drinks, fine wine, and many beers, to our all inclusive, nonalocoholic beverages such as soda, juice, sparkling water, is sure to quench your thirst.
