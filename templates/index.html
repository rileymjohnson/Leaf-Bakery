About Leaf

Leaf is a Five Star high-end restaurant serving you only the best food. We offer a wide variety of options from healthy and delicious soups and salads, to your not so healthy, but still delicious desserts. We have won many awards spread throughout our eight menu categories. We even offer a Kids Menu which is sure to satisfy your children's cravings!

Totally Gluten-Free

We promise to serve a completely gluten-free menu. From our pertfectly crafted appetizers and entrees, to our wonderful looking and tasting desserts. Our specialty drink menu offers many wonderful gluten-free options. Our smoothies are all homemade to ensure they are gluten-free and taste the best then can. We fresh squeeze all our juices right in the kitchen, and brew the tea with only the finest water!

Only Organic Foods

All of our dishes are made from organically grown foods. We buy all the vegetables and fruits we need from a local farmers market and we buy the ingredients to make bread from a local grainery. This is another way we pledge our support to being environmentally friendly and supporting local businesses. We also never use GMOs or any other chemical that would alter the food. Our goal is to provide delicious and healthy food to customers.
