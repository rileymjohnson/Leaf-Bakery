About Our Chef

Here at Leaf Bakery our head chef, Chef Antonio Morencho, crafts some of the finest food you will ever eat. He is an expert at cooking gluten-free, and has been doing it for his entire career. He always delivers the highest quality of foods. Everyday before work Chef Morencho goes to the farmers market and buys fresh meat and veggies. He always makes sure the food is cooked to perfection, and never lets food below par out of the kitchen. He frequently makes trips to the dining room, and socializes with customers, making sure they feel like they are at home.

In his free time, Antonio loves to spend time with his family. He loves to play catch with his kids, and watch romantic movies with his wife. During the summer, he takes his family to their lake house, where they fish and boat.
